Privacy Policy

CosFlow - Focus & Productivity App

Last Updated: January 2025

Simple Summary: CosFlow does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device. Your privacy is 100% protected.

Information We Collect

None. CosFlow does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics from users.

Data Storage

All focus session data, including your Focus Score, session history, and statistics, are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Is not backed up to cloud services by CosFlow
  • Can only be accessed by you through the app

Network Communication

CosFlow does not communicate with any external servers. The app functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Third-Party Services

CosFlow does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not integrate with:

  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud backup services
  • Any other third-party data collection tools

User Accounts

CosFlow does not require user accounts, email addresses, or any form of registration. You can use the app completely anonymously.

Location Data

CosFlow does not access, collect, or use your location data in any way.

Device Permissions

CosFlow only requests the following system permission:

  • Prevent Sleep: To keep your device awake during active focus sessions

No other permissions are requested or required.

Children's Privacy

CosFlow is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk to children's privacy. The app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted, your focus session data is as secure as your device itself. We recommend:

  • Using a device passcode or biometric authentication
  • Keeping your iOS software up to date
  • Backing up your device regularly (if you want to preserve your focus data)

Data Deletion

To delete all your CosFlow data, simply delete the app from your device. All session history and Focus Score data will be permanently removed.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Any changes will be reflected in app updates on the App Store.

Your Rights

Since CosFlow does not collect any personal data, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your data through the app itself.

California Privacy Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), CosFlow does not sell, share, or process personal information, so CCPA disclosure requirements do not apply.

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, CosFlow is fully compliant with GDPR regulations because we do not process any personal data.
